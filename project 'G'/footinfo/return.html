Returns Policy

Easy and hassle-free returns process

30-Day Return Policy

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Ensure your item meets our return criteria

Step 2: Initiate Return

Start the return process through your account

Step 3: Ship Item

Package and ship the item back to us

Eligibility for Returns

Items must meet the following criteria to be eligible for return:

  • Unused and in original condition
  • In original packaging with all accessories
  • Include original receipt or proof of purchase
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery

How to Return an Item

  1. Log in to your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Select the item you wish to return
  3. Choose the reason for return
  4. Print the return label and packing slip
  5. Package the item securely
  6. Ship the item using the provided return label

Refund Process

Once we receive your return:

  • We will inspect the returned item
  • Process your refund within 3-5 business days
  • Refund will be issued to your original payment method
  • You will receive an email confirmation

Note: Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Digital products and downloads
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Items without original packaging

Contact Us: If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact our customer service team.
